# Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## 1. Detailed Pricing Strategy for Kenya Market

### Market Analysis
- **Target Customers**: 50,000+ expats in Kenya
- **Average Household Income**: $3,000-15,000/month
- **Domestic Worker Cost**: $80-200/month per worker
- **Market Size**: Estimated $2.4M ARR potential

### Pricing Tiers

#### Free Tier
- **Price**: $0/month
- **Features**: 
  - 1 domestic worker profile
  - Basic payment tracking
  - Simple time logging
  - Basic tax calculations
- **Limitations**: No M-Pesa integration, manual payments only
- **Purpose**: User acquisition and product demonstration

#### Basic Tier
- **Price**: $15/month or $150/year (17% discount)
- **Features**:
  - Up to 3 domestic workers
  - M-Pesa bulk payments
  - Basic leave tracking
  - PAYE/NSSF/NHIF calculations
  - SMS notifications
  - Email support
- **Target**: Single expat households

#### Professional Tier
- **Price**: $45/month or $450/year (17% discount)
- **Features**:
  - Up to 10 domestic workers
  - Advanced HR management
  - Performance tracking
  - Contract management
  - In-app messaging
  - Multiple property support
  - Priority support (24h response)
  - KRA iTax integration
- **Target**: Larger households, small businesses

#### Enterprise Tier
- **Price**: $120/month or $1,200/year (17% discount)
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited workers
  - White-label options
  - API access
  - Custom workflows
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Training sessions
  - SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
  - Advanced analytics
- **Target**: Property management companies, NGOs

### Freemium vs Free Trial Analysis

#### Recommendation: Hybrid Approach
- **Free Tier**: Permanent with limitations
- **Paid Tiers**: 14-day free trial with full features
- **Reasoning**: 
  - Free tier for market penetration
  - Trials for conversion optimization
  - Reduces friction for premium features
