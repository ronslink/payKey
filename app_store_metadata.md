## Description
Simplify your life with PayDome, the ultimate payroll management solution designed specifically for employers of domestic staff in Kenya.

Managing wages for your nanny, housekeeper, gardener, or driver shouldn't be a headache. PayDome automates the entire process, ensuring accurate calculations, timely payments, and professional record-keeping.

**Key Features:**

*   **Automated Payroll**: Say goodbye to manual calculations. We handle all the math for you, including allowances and deductions.
*   **Instant Payslips**: Generate professional, itemized payslips automatically every month. Keep digital records for transparency and peace of mind.
*   **Worker Management**: Maintain detailed profiles for all your staff, including contact details, roles, and payment history in one secure dashboard.
*   **Seamless Payments**: (Coming Soon) Integrate directly with mobile money services to disburse salaries with a single tap.
*   **Tax Ready**: Stay organized with data that helps you stay compliant with local regulations.

Whether you have one employee or a full household team, PayDome saves you time and brings professionalism to your home management.
