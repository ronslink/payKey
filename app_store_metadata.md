## Description
PayDome is a payroll management application designed for employers of domestic staff in Kenya.

PayDome helps calculate payroll, organize records, prepare payslips, and track supported payments for a nanny, housekeeper, gardener, driver, or other domestic worker.

**Key Features:**

*   **Automated Payroll**: Say goodbye to manual calculations. We handle all the math for you, including allowances and deductions.
*   **Instant Payslips**: Generate professional, itemized payslips automatically every month. Keep digital records for transparency and peace of mind.
*   **Worker Management**: Maintain detailed profiles for your staff, including contact details, roles, and payment history in one dashboard.
*   **Flexible Payment Records**: Record cash payroll and, where payment services are enabled for your account, initiate supported electronic payments.
*   **Kenya Statutory Calculations**: Calculate PAYE, NSSF, SHIF, and Affordable Housing Levy using rates effective for the payroll date.
*   **Tax Records**: Keep organized payroll data and reports to support your filing workflow.

Whether you have one employee or a full household team, PayDome saves you time and brings professionalism to your home management.

PayDome provides payroll calculations and records; employers remain responsible for reviewing and filing statutory obligations.
