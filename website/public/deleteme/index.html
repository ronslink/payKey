Delete Account & Data

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly from the mobile application.

Instructions:

  1. Open the PayDome App on your device.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Profile.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Upon confirmation, all your personal data, payroll records, and history will be permanently queued for deletion in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

If you cannot access the app, please contact support@paydome.co for assistance.
